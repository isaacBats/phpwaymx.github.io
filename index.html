About

The event is non-profit and organized by the same group of volunteers that brought Drupal Camp, Symfony Day, and PHP Day to Mexico City in the most recent years.


Over the years, we have acquired new learning and understanding of the developers in Mexico, and we think it's about time we produced Mexico's first ever, PHP Conference focused solely on developers who want to reach the next level in their careers!

Schedule

PHP Mexico Developer Conference is an exciting, new, 2 day event for developers who want to dive deep into advanced engineering and development topics centered around PHP.

Code of Conduct
PHPMX is committed to creating a conference that is as inclusive as possible. We want to showcase talent from around the world, and from all groups. We are also committed to ensuring the conference is a place where ideas are exchanged, old friends get together, new friends meet, and harassment of any kind is not tolerated. If you experience any problems of this nature at the event please contact one of the event organizers.
